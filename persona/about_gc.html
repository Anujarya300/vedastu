MR. G.C GARG

B.E.(CIVIL) – IIT ROORKEE, MIE, F.I.V, CHARTERED ENGINEER & GOVT. OF INDIA APPROVED VALUER

About Me

B. E. (Civil) from IIT Roorkee (erstwhile University of Roorkee). His professional experience includes Valuation & Planning, budgeting, monitoring and construction of all types of buildings (Residential, Commercial & Industrial shed) including internal development work such as water supply, waste & sewage disposal, landscaping, horticulture and road works along with administrative works related to class-I officers of Uttar Pradesh State Construction and Infrastructure Development Corp. (Formerly U.P. Samaj Kalyan Nirman Nigam Ltd.). He is also registered under section 34AB of Wealth Tax Act, 1957 for Valuation of Immovable Properties.

He has having vast experience of civil contract management and cost estimate preparation. He has served the above organization (a Govt. of U.P. Enterprise) for the period of about 35 years and retired from the post of Regional Manager in the year 2015.
